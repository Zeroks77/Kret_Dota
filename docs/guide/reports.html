Reports & viewers

Pick a report (month/patch/league) and then use the viewers inside the page to answer common questions.

Report types

  • Monthly reports – month-based snapshots.
  • Patch reports – aggregated by game patch.
  • User reports – temporary user-focused filters.
  • League reports – competition-specific wrappers.

Viewers

Players

Who performed best? Sort by KDA, GPM/XPM, or participation.

Heroes

Which heroes dominated? See win rates and popularity.

Draft

What mattered in draft? Compare phases, picks, and bans.

Lane duos

Which lanes worked? Identify strong pairings and outcomes.

Wards

Where were the key wards? Explore placements on the map.

Keyboard model

  • Main tab shortcuts: 1–0 activate tabs where available.
  • Tablists: use ←/→ to move focus, Home/End to jump; Enter/Space to activate.
  • Tables: headers toggled with Enter/Space, aria-sort reflects state; captions aid screen readers.
  • Wards flyout: opens on Enter/Space; Esc closes and returns focus to opener.

Permalinks

Use the “Copy link” or “Share” actions in viewers. The link remembers your current filters and selections. See Permalinks & Embeds for all supported URL parameters and examples.
