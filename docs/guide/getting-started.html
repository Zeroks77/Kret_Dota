Getting started

1) Open the reports

Go to the homepage of reports at docs/index.html. Pick a month or a league.

2) Use the tabs

Switch between Players, Heroes, Draft, Lane duos, and Wards. Sort columns to compare.

3) Share your view

Use “Copy link” to share exactly what you see. The link remembers your filters and selections.

Troubleshooting

  • Data looks old? Check the “Last updated” banner on the Roadmap page or refresh the browser.
  • Table won’t sort? Try tapping the header again; on small screens, some columns are hidden.
  • Map not responding? Ensure “Reduce motion” isn’t blocking animations; try refreshing the page.
