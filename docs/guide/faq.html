Frequently asked questions

Where do I find the latest monthly report?

Open the homepage and click the month you want. The “Last updated” banner shows when the data was refreshed.

How often is the data updated?

Monthly reports are typically updated at least once a month. League pages update when new matches are ingested. Check the Roadmap page’s banner for the latest build time.

How can I share a specific view?

Use the “Copy link” or “Share” buttons in viewers. These copy a permalink that remembers filters and selections.

Some tables look different on my phone. Is that normal?

Yes. On small screens we collapse less‑important columns so the most important info remains readable and tappable.

Can I export data to CSV?

Some league wrappers include CSV links; more exports are on the roadmap. You can also copy tables manually from the browser.

Keyboard tips?
  • Tab to move between controls
  • Enter/Space to activate
  • Arrow keys to move inside tablists
  • Esc to close dialogs
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Add an item on the Roadmap or open a GitHub issue. Screenshots and steps to reproduce help.

What about privacy?

Reports summarize publicly available match data. No personal logins or tracking cookies are required.

Which browsers are supported?

Recent versions of Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. Mobile Safari is supported with small-screen adjustments.

What if the data looks wrong?

Data comes from OpenDota. If you spot an issue, open an item on the Roadmap or create an issue in the repository.
